Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to the school?

To apply to our school, visit the "Apply" section on our website and fill out the application form. Once submitted, our admissions team will review your application.

What are the school's operating hours?

The school operates from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.
